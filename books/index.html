A princess is kidnapped and turned into a bloodthirsty monster.
Can she reclaim her throne—and her humanity?

Margaret, the last heir to the Scottish throne, is betrayed on her way to safety in England. Two Scotsmen steal her away to a dark castle where she joins a clan of redcapes—creatures who soak their capes in blood to survive. The castle is infiltrated by redcape hunters and Margaret discovers she is a sorceress, or a heart-taker, and sorcery may be the way to free herself from her cape and reclaim the life she was meant to have.

Gudrun, a sorceress in Burgundy and Margaret's ancestor, would like nothing more than to ease her guilty conscience. She kills and regrets, and her mother makes her forget—it's a cycle Gudrun can't escape, until she meets a man who brings light to her dark world. With him, she's happy, but her happiness is short-lived. She wakes to find him murdered and devotes her life to resurrecting her one true love.

Pitched as Outlander meets Grave Mercy but with more gore and less romance, Hearts Like Silver is a dark, historical fantasy that explores themes of forgiveness, hope and freedom.

Hearts Like Silver is slated for publication in 2024. The cover shown is a stand-in cover.
